# Product Plan

## Positioning

Nurture is a warm fertility companion for iOS users who are trying to conceive. It combines period and fertility logging, HealthKit sync, AI coaching, and subscription-based deeper insights.

## MVP

- Local-first cycle records: period, symptoms, basal body temperature, LH tests, intercourse, supplements.
- Today dashboard: cycle day, fertile window, tasks, quick logging, AI guidance.
- AI coach: consent-scoped context, structured output, non-diagnostic safety boundaries.
- Insights: cycle regularity, fertile-window confidence, temperature and LH trends.
- Profile: cloud sync, AI data consent, HealthKit permissions, subscription management, data controls.

## Out of Scope for v1

- Doctor dashboard.
- Community features.
- Medical diagnosis or medication recommendations.
- Advertising data sharing.

## Subscription

Free users get core logging, HealthKit access, and limited daily AI messages. Pro users get higher AI limits, cloud sync, deep reports, and longitudinal trend summaries.
